The Harry Potter series, by J.K. Rowling

Harry Potter is the young protagonist of this series. Living with his non-magical aunt, uncle, and bully cousin, he must learn to navigate the complex world of wizardy, of which he is a part. This is made harder by his family's fear of magic, school rivalries, and an enemy intent on taking over the wizarding world - an enemy who killed his parents.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
Harry Potter is a wizard, and he goes to the best wizarding school around: Hogwarts. Harry inheirts family disputes, makes new friends, and faces a villian from his past. During the summer an impish creature warns Harry that returning to the school would bring about disaster. He goes back to the despite, and students are being attacked. There are 3 suspects, which includes Harry himself! A criminal has broken out the wizard prison, Azkaban, and seems to be hunting Harry! Harry must unravel a mystery that revels secrets over 13 years old. Harry is entered into the Triwizard Tournament, and must face dangerous challenges and determined enemies. In the end, there's more than gold on the line. Harry's life is in the balance!
Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix Harry Potter and the Halfblood Prince Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows The Cursed Child
Harry learns about the mysterious Order of the Phoenix, an orginization designed to stop You-Know-Who, the villan trying to take over the wizarding world, and kill Harry! An old spellbook filled with homemade spells falls into Harry's hands, and he juggles learning what the spells are, and leaving the school on special missions to stop You-Know-Who Harry is seeking the Deathly Hallows, 3 items said to help the user conquer death. Harry must fight You-Know-Who in a final battle, and determine the fate of the world The Cursed Child is a play set 19 years later, revolving around Harry's son, Albus. The Percy Jackson series, by Rick Riordan

Percy Jackson is continuasly kicked out of boarding school. But that's not his fault. Mythological monsters and Greek gods have started leaveing the pages of textbooks, and trying to kill him. Percy must enter a new world, full of monsters and magic, and unravel a treachery more powerful than the gods themselves.

Percy Jackson - The Lightning Thief Percy Jackson - The Sea of Monsters Percy Jackson - The Titan's Curse Percy Jackson - The Battle of the Labyrinth Percy Jackson - The Last Olympian
Percy is suspected of stealing the property of one of the gods. He has only a few days to return it, or his head is on the line. Percy goes on another quest to help his friend, Grover, who's in trouble. The only problem is that he broke the rules by leaving, and is now running from his friends. Percy's friend, Annabeth goes missing, so Percy sneaks out of camp again to try and save her. He's forced to bear the weight of the sky, and sacrifice friends. Percy and his friends must navigate a trecherous, self-aware maze intent on killing him. If he doesn't, then the camp is doomed. The final battle has arrived, and Percy must fight the evil, titan Kronos. If the loses, then the entire world will fall under the control of the titan lord.
